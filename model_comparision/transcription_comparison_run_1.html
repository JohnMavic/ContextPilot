ðŸ’¡ Conclusion & Recommendation

OpenAI gpt-4o-mini-transcribe delivers the best results among the tested models with 72% accuracy, capturing complete text with acceptable word accuracy despite some duplications and proper noun errors. Azure gpt-4o-mini-transcribe performs worst at 52%, suffering from severe sentence fragmentation (12 grammar/structure errors), critical word substitutions ("identity app", "Foundation", "MTP", "deep learning"), and hallucinations at the end. Azure gpt-4o-transcribe scores 58% but loses ~20% of content through missing text, making it less suitable for complete transcriptions. For production use, OpenAI's direct API consistently outperforms Azure OpenAI for transcription tasks. Manual post-processing remains essential for all models.
